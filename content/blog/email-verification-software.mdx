#### What is email verification software?

![](/blog-images/646260736b63336c71357ff7_1tcb8n9-1024x576.webp)

Email verification software helps you evaluate whether an email address is active or invalid. It lets you keep your email lists healthy, experience low bounce rates, and maintain a good sender reputation.

The email verification service includes the following steps:

-   **Syntax errors check** to identify the email validity via four components – user name, address sign, subdomain, and top-level domain
-   **Blacklist check** to inspect an email list against spam traps, bots, and other fraudulent emails
-   **Domain MX records check to determine whether MX records are legitimate and can accept the domain’s email messages**
-   **Using Simple Mail Transfer Protocol (SMTP)** to ping a mail server and determine whether given email addresses are disabled, active, or nonexistent

The email verification tools come in two forms – real-time email verification API and bulk verification. With the right email validation service, you will see bounce rates decreasing, [consumer trust rising](https://hbr.org/2022/11/3-strategies-to-earn-consumer-trust-in-email-marketing), and more verified leads coming your way.
