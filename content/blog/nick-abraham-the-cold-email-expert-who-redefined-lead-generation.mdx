## The Story Behind Nick’s Success

Nick’s journey into cold email marketing started with **a vision: to simplify and optimize lead generation for businesses, particularly in the B2B space**. With a deep understanding of how traditional methods were inefficient, Nick founded **Leadbird.io**. His success is marked not only by his ability to stay ahead of digital marketing trends but by his hands-on approach to refining the cold email process for each client.

Read [here](https://www.ocean.io/case-studies/leadbird) what Nick has to say about how Leadbird improves cold email efficiency.

**Leadbird.io**, positioned itself as a premier lead generation platform, offering advanced cold email automation and prospecting services that streamline outreach efforts for businesses of all sizes. 

Later, Nick co-founded **Scrubby.io** with [Erik Paulson](https://scrubby.io/founders/), and [Anirudh Kaushal](https://scrubby.io/founders/), a game-changing **complete email validation tool** that goes beyond traditional methods. Scrubby’s unique ability to detect risky and catch-all emails has set a new standard for email list hygiene, helping businesses achieve nearly flawless deliverability rates. 

So this became the perfect combination, this means that businesses using **Leadbird.io** aren’t just sending more emails—they’re sending smarter emails to **verified, reliable recipients using Scrubby.** 

For businesses using Leadbird.io, Scrubby is the invisible additional layover of force that ensures every email campaign is built on a foundation of clean, validated data. It allows Nick to scale Leadbird.io’s services without compromising on the quality and effectiveness of their email outreach.

_“…With Scrubby, users can now validate these catch-all and risky emails, a capability that was not available in the market previously. This innovation allows businesses to reclaim and effectively utilize 40 to 60% of their email list that they would have otherwise considered unusable…” –_ [_Nick_](https://cloudtask.com/content-themes/sales-software-reviews/nick-abraham-scrubby-104/)

Nick’s journey into the world of email validation and lead generation is impressive, not just because of his successful ventures, but also because of the leadership and insights he offers to the wider community. He is known for being a thought leader, regularly sharing his strategies and knowledge on platforms like X (formerly Twitter) and LinkedIn.
