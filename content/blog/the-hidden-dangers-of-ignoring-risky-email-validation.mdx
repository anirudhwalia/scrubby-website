## **What is Email Validation?**

Before we jump into the deep end, let’s clarify exactly what we mean by email validation. Essentially, email validation is a process that verifies whether an email address is valid and can successfully receive messages. Think of it as a quality assurance step for your email list. This step can include syntax checking, domain verification, and even directly pinging the email server to ensure it exists and is receptive.

## **The Risks of Ignoring Email Validation**

#### **Increased Bounce Rates**

One of the most immediate consequences of ignoring email validation is a spike in email bounce rates. When you send emails to invalid addresses, they bounce back, indicating that the delivery failed. This not only wastes your marketing efforts but also tarnishes your sender reputation. Internet Service Providers (ISPs) monitor these metrics and a high bounce rate can lead to your emails being flagged as spam, or worse, being blacklisted.

With [Scrubby](https://scrubby.io/), you can mitigate this risk. Its comprehensive validation checks reduce the chances of sending emails to invalid addresses, ensuring that your messages reach real inboxes.

#### **Damaged Sender Reputation**

Your sender reputation is like your credit score in the eyes of ISPs. It’s crucial for ensuring that your emails land in inboxes rather than spam folders. **A poor sender reputation can dramatically lower your email deliverability rates, meaning your carefully crafted marketing messages might never reach your audience**. Over time, this can negatively affect your brand image, customer relationships, and even sales figures.

Scrubby helps protect your sender reputation by validating emails thoroughly, ensuring that your email campaigns maintain high deliverability and reach your target audience effectively.

#### **Exposure to Spam Traps**

Spam traps are email addresses specifically created to catch spammers. They do not belong to real users and serve no other purpose than to identify unsolicited emails. When you send emails to these addresses, you risk being flagged as a spammer. Avoiding risky email validation increases the chances of encountering these traps, potentially putting your entire email marketing campaign at risk.

Scrubby’s unique approach includes identifying risky and catch-all emails, significantly reducing the chances of your emails landing in spam traps.

#### **Increased Costs**

Sending emails to invalid addresses not only harms your reputation but also costs you money. Most email service providers (ESPs) charge based on the number of emails sent or the size of your contact list. **If a significant portion of your list is invalid, you’re essentially throwing money down the drain.** Further, the cost to rectify a damaged sender reputation can also be substantial, involving more complex validation procedures and consultations.
