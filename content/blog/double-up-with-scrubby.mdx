###### Boost Your Campaigns with Double the Credits This New Year!

##### As we welcome 2024, Scrubby is excited to offer a unique opportunity to supercharge your email marketing efforts. Scrubby’s “Double Up” campaign ensures that your emails make twice the impact with double the validation power. Use your doubled credits alongside [Kali](https://getkali.io) for calendar invite outreach that converts validated leads into booked demos, and keep tabs on competitor promotions with [CAM](https://getcam.io) to stay ahead this season.

## **“Double Up”** Campaign Official Terms & Conditions

### Campaign Duration:

-   Start Date: January 1, 2024
-   End Date: January 31, 2024

### Campaign Terms:

-   Eligibility:
    -   The “Double Up” offer is open to new sign-ups and existing Scrubby customers.

-   Double Credits Offer:
    -   During the campaign period, customers will receive double the credits for any credit package purchased. For instance, buying a 1,000 email credit package will entitle the customer to 1,000 credits at no extra cost.

-   Activation:
    -   Credits will automatically be doubled upon purchase during the campaign period. No promo code is required.

-   Usage Period:
    -   The doubled credits will be valid for 90 days from the date of purchase, expiring at the end of March.

-   Maximum Limit:
    -   The offer is capped at a maximum of 100,000 credits per customer.

-   Exclusivity:
    -   This offer cannot be combined with other promotions or discounts Scrubby offers.

-   Applicable Payment Models:
    -   The offer is valid across all payment models, including Pay As You Go and monthly subscription plans.

-   Non-Refundable:
    -   All purchases made under the “Double Up” campaign are final and non-refundable.

-   Right to Modify/Cancel:
    -   Scrubby reserves the right to modify, cancel, or limit the offer at any time without prior notice,

-   Compliance with Laws:
    -   Participants must ensure compliance with local laws and regulations

-   Support and Queries:
    -   For any questions or assistance regarding the campaign, please contact our support team at info@scrubby.io.
