## **The “Risky” Email Dilemma**

What exactly makes an email “risky”? Traditional validation tools flag emails as risky when they:

-   Belong to catch-all domains
-   Have complex server setups
-   Are potentially disposable addresses
-   Show patterns that might lead to hard bounces
-   Could impact sender reputation

Here’s the crucial insight: Up to 30% of emails in B2B databases get flagged as “risky” by standard validation tools. However, our data shows that up to 80% of these supposedly risky emails are actually valuable leads. This means businesses following traditional validation advice might be throwing away a significant portion of their potential revenue.
